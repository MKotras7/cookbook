## Ingredients

* 2 cups heavy whipping cream
* 1/2 cups fresh grated parmesan
* Fettuccine

## Instructions

### Cook Alfredo

### Make Sauce

* Pour cream into a pan and bring to simmer
* Slowly combine parmesan until combined
* Continue to simmer until desired thickness
* Combine pasta
* Serve
